# 第四章：核心词汇突破

## 四级高频词汇（1）

### 动词类

| 单词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| acquire | 获得；习得 | She acquired a lot of knowledge from books. |
| achieve | 达到；完成 | He achieved his goal through hard work. |
| advocate | 提倡；拥护 | Many doctors advocate regular exercise. |
| analyze | 分析 | We need to analyze the data carefully. |
| allocate | 分配；拨出 | The government allocated funds for education. |

### 形容词类

| 单词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| accessible | 可接近的；易懂的 | The building is accessible to wheelchairs. |
| accurate | 准确的；精确的 | The experiment gave accurate results. |
| abundant | 丰富的；充裕的 | The region has abundant natural resources. |
| adaptable | 适应力强的 | Children are highly adaptable. |
| advantageous | 有利的；有益的 | This arrangement is advantageous to both sides. |

## 记忆技巧

### 词根词缀法

- **spec**（看）→ inspect（向内看=检查）、respect（反复看=尊重）、specify（详细说明）
- **port**（携带）→ transport（运输）、export（出口）、portable（便携的）
- **struct**（建造）→ construct（建造）、structure（结构）、destruct（破坏）

### 联想记忆法

把生词和你已经知道的词联系起来：
- **ambiguous**（模棱两可的）≈ ambiguous（amb- = 双 + ious）→ 有两种解释 → 模糊不清
- **bene-**（好）+ **vol**（意愿）→ **benevolent**（仁慈的）
